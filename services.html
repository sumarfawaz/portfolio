Websites



"We have different types of websites that are based on different requirements and needs. Although you have a picture of what your website should look like, you should also choose the correct type of website to accomplish your goals. We will walk you through different types of websites that are widely used by businesses and individuals".

-Portfolio-

It is becoming increasingly necessary for artists, engineers, computer scientists, photographers, designers, filmmakers, and other creative individuals to have a means of displaying and promoting their work. Regardless of the medium(s) you choose, a portfolio will provide more individuals with access to your work and a better understanding of your talents. They present abilities and initiatives in a strong and aesthetically appealing manner that complements the material in your resume and cover letter. They also allow you to arrange your work in one location, even if it spans other fields. Companies are actively developing their portfolios in order to attract more clients to purchase their services and products, which will eventually be transformed into e-commerce websites.



-Website as a Service (WaaS)-

Website as a Service (WaaS) encompasses the comprehensive design, development, maintenance, and technical elements necessary to create an industry-specific and customized website. The (WaaS) package includes a large number of components. The package includes everything you need to build the ideal web presence for your company.



-Informational Websites-

Because the major focus of these sites is the content, informational websites as a design can serve a variety of objectives, ranging from personal usage to small enterprises to bigger organizations. An informational website is definitely suitable for you if you want to develop a basic online presence and share your unique material.



-Blog-

Blogs are also presented nearly conversationally. They are often casual and may be easily run with a little practice. Corporations may also publish blogs in order to generate informational material or to establish themselves as authorities in their field. These are used by brands and YouTube influencers to increase revenue for a product or service.



-Web Portal-

In a nutshell, a web portal is a web-based platform that allows workers, customers, and suppliers to access information from a single location. A web portal can be used to give tailored information to the user, such as employee training, safety guidelines, or a consumer profile. A web portal may also be used to increase information cooperation and the way workers, customers, and suppliers connect with your company.



-E-Commerce-

By definition, an e-commerce website is one that allows you to purchase and sell actual commodities, digital products, or services online. Trade, whether via barter exchange or the buying and selling of products and services, has existed for ages. Nobody is self-sufficient. This highlights the importance of demand and supply for products and services.




Domain and Hosting

"Domain and Hosting are considered the most important procedure in web development. The domain is a string recognition to your website instead of an IP address which is too hard to remember. Hosting is the process of getting your website loaded into a server that runs 24/7. We will do the needful to get you a domain and load your website into a server".

Linux Hosting

Hosting websites on open-source Linux Servers is known as Linux hosting. Almost all the servers on the internet use Linux distros because it is easy to manage and the level of security is way better than any other server operating system. Linux shared hosting is the cheapest type of web hosting because you share the same Linux server with more than one user and the hardware specs of the server are divided among the users themselves. They have to pay a shared amount and it costs less is suitable for a small-scale startup website.

VPS Hosting

VPS (Virtual Private Server) is the concept where you get a virtual server under your control and you govern and set rules to your website and there is no third-party access or software maintenance unless you request maintenance. VPS uses virtualization to set up a virtual server on another physical server which you can access and work on your website and much more remotely. This is a hosting plan for medium-scale websites that are willing to move to a large scale. VPSs are quite expensive and several other hosting servers are above the tier of a VPS (Virtual Private Server).

Dedicated Server Hosting

A dedicated server (or servers) is a type of Internet hosting in which a physical server (or servers) is devoted to a single business customer. The customer gets total control over the computer, allowing them to configure it for their specific needs, such as performance and security.

Managed Server Hosting

Managed hosting is an IT provisioning model in which a service provider leases dedicated servers and associated hardware to a single client and administers those systems on behalf of that customer. Customers can rent equipment such as dedicated server, storage, and network hardware, as well as operating systems and system software, through managed hosting.

SEO

"SEO is known as search engine optimization which makes a website stand out from other websites that provide the same service or sell the same commodity".


Search Engine Optimization is a very important aspect after the website is created and deployed on the internet. Search engine optimization is the process of making your website appear in the first set of pages and on the top as much as possible so that you can get more traffic to your website. Even though you provide an exceptional service at a very cost-effective or without a cost, without proper SEO your website wont stand up on the list. Traffic refers to clients or ends users who visit your website. The concept behind SEO is that make your website familiar to the search engine so that whenever a user searches for something related to your website the search engine brings you to the top because more clients have visited the particular website for this search and your website comes to the top. When you dont have proper SEO, your website will remain unseen or noticed and you wont generate any traffic or profit. This is why SEO is very much important to make a website successful.

There are two types of SEO, which are paid and organic. Paid SEO is done by search engines and whenever a particular keyword or search matches your website you will be at the top of the list. The second type of SEO is organic which is done by different practices and constant work and time invested in it. According to surveys, organic SEO works way better than paid SEO.
